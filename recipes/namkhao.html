Nam Khao

Description

A crispy rice salad with red curry, fresh herbs, and sour pork. Served cold and eaten like a lettuce wrap.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Lemon Grass
  • Kaffir Lime Leaves
  • Ginger
  • Desiccated Coconut
  • Red Curry Paste
  • Scallions
  • Sugar
  • Fish Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Cooked Rice
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Fried Chilies
  • Sour Pork

Steps

  1. Mix rice, red curry paste, minced garlic, lemon grass, kaffir lime leaves ginger, coconut, sugar, fish sauce, and eggs.
  2. Form in to balls the size of an egg, then deep fry until brown color
  3. When cooled, break up rice ball in bowl and then toss with scallions, cilantro, chilies, sour pork and lime.
  4. Serve with Lettuce.
