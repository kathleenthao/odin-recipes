Kao Niao (Sticky Rice)

Description

Glutinous rice that is soaked, then steamed. It is served as side carbohydrate served with almost every Lao meal.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Glutinous Rice

Steps

  1. Soak rice in water for at least 3 hours, but better over night.
  2. In bamboo stead, add soaked rice and steam until top of rice is translucent.
  3. When translucent, flip rice to cook and additional 5-10 minutes.
  4. Spread rice on bamboo mat to cool slightly, then serve in bamboo basket.
