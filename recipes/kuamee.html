Kua Mee

Description

A sweet and savory Lao fried noodle dish, typically served at parties.

Ingredients

  • Rice Nodles
  • Eggs
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Sugar
  • Sweet Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Seasoning Sauce
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Fried Chiles
  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water, chop shallots, garlic, scallions.
  2. Caramelize sugar in oil until brown, add shallots and garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add all 3 sauces and mix, then add drain rice noodles and toss until noodles are cooked.
  4. Remove noodles on to tray and fry up omelet, then cool and slice omelet.
  5. Separate noodles when cooled, then toss with omelet slices, scallions, bean sprouts, and fried chiles. Serve.
